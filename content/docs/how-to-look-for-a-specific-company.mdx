---
title: How to look for a specific company
description: Easily search for any company or domain on TheirStack. View their technologies and job listings with a simple click.
---

There is a search box in the header of TheirStack where you can look for any company or its domain.

![Image 1](https://images.prismic.io/theirstack/Zk4KZCol0Zci9W_Q_CleanShot2024-05-22at17.03.57.gif?auto=format,compress)When you click on one of them, a modal will open on the right side of your screen. Let's take Deutsche Bank for example:

![Image 2](https://images.prismic.io/theirstack/Zk34ZSol0Zci9Ww2_CleanShot2024-05-22at15.51.12.png?auto=format,compress)For every company, you'll see that there are 2 buttons:

*   click on **Technologies** to take show you the technologies of that company ([see more here](https://theirstack.com/en/docs/how-to-see-the-technologies-of-a-company))
*   click on **Jobs** to see the jobs from this company
